Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

At Plantora, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and name.
  • Plant Data: Information about the plants you add, including photos, names, and care schedules.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how you interact with the app to help us improve performance.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide personalized care reminders for your plants.
  • To analyze plant health using our AI algorithms.
  • To maintain and improve the App's functionality.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored on Supabase servers using industry-standard encryption. We verify that our service providers adhere to strict security standards. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Camera & Photo Library

We request access to your device's camera and photo library solely for the purpose of plant identification and health analysis. The photos you take are processed to provide these services and are stored securely associated with your account.

Third-Party AI Services & Data Sharing

Our app uses OpenAI, a third-party artificial intelligence service, to analyze plant photos for identification and health diagnosis. When you choose to use the "Identify" or "Analyze" features:

  • Your plant photo is securely transmitted to OpenAI's API.
  • The data sent is used solely for the purpose of generating the analysis result.
  • We do not share your personal contact information or other unrelated account details with OpenAI.
  • We have configured our usage to ensure your data is not used to train OpenAI's models.

You will always be asked for explicit confirmation before any photo is sent for external analysis.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can delete your account directly within the app settings, which will permanently remove all your data from our servers.
